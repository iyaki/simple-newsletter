Simple Newsletter

Atom & RSS Feeds to Newsletter

The news that you want to hear about, delivered directly to your inbox

Insert the URI of the Atom or RSS feed that you want to convert to newsletter and the e-mail address where you want to receive it.

Why?

I have used RSS Feeds for a long time and I completely love them. I use them, advocate for them and try to spread the word about them.

But beggining the 2024 I came across this post and it left me thinking.

It's true that for a lot of the readers of new generations the RSS is an old and strange technology that they will never use. But for the publishers the RSS (or Atom) is almost omnipresent without any effort, unlike newsletters.

So I decided to build this service, allowing any reader to subscribe through a an e-mail address to any Atom or RSS feed in a newsletter format.

F.A.Q.

  • How do I subscribe?

    Just fill out the form with the URI of the Atom or RSS feed and your e-mail address and submit it. The service will send you a confirmation e-mail to your inbox.

  • How do I unsubscribe?

    On every newsletter that you receive there is a link to unsubscribe from it.

  • Is this service free?

    Yes!

  • I'm a publisher, can I use offer this service to my subscribers through a form in my web page?

    Of course!. There is documentation on how to do it.

  • Something is wrong

    Please, let me know
